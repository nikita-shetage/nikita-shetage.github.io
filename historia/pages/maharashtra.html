Sindhudurg Fort –
Sindhudurg

Info: A sea fort built by Shivaji Maharaj, known for its hidden escape routes and historic cannons. Opening Hours: 8:00 AM – 5:30 PM Entry Fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners)

Book Now

Pratapgad Fort –
Satara

Info: The site of the famous Battle of Pratapgad between Shivaji and Afzal Khan in 1659. Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM Entry Fee: Free

Gingee Fort – Villupuram

Info: A massive fort known as the "Troy of the East," built by the Cholas and later expanded by the Marathas. Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM Entry Fee: ₹25 (Indians), ₹300 (Foreigners)

Book Now

Dindigul Fort – Dindigul

Info: A historic hill fort with connections to Tipu Sultan and the British. Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:30 PM Entry Fee: ₹20 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners)

Ramanathaswamy Temple – Rameswaram

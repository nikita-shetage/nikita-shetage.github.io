Monuments & Heritage sites

Hawa Mahal – Jaipur


Info: "Palace of Winds," built for royal women to observe city life from behind intricate jharokhas (windows). Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 4:30 PM Entry Fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹200 (Foreigners)


Book Now

City Palace – Udaipur

Info: A stunning palace complex overlooking Lake Pichola, showcasing Mewar royal heritage and a museum with artifacts. Opening Hours: 9:30 AM – 5:30 PM Entry Fee: ₹300 (Indians), ₹800 (Foreigners)

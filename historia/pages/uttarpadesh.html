Agra Fort–
Agra

Info: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a grand Mughal fort that served as the main residence of the emperors. Opening Hours: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM Entry Fee: ₹50 (Indians), ₹650 (Foreigners)

Mirzapur

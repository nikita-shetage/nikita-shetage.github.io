Info: One of the largest pilgrimage sites in the world, dedicated to Lord Ayyappa. Opening Hours: 3:00 AM – 12:00 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM (Seasonal Opening) Entry Fee: Free

Guruvayur Temple – Thrissur

Info: A revered Krishna temple, famous for its strict rituals and elephant sanctuary. Opening Hours: 3:00 AM – 12:30 PM, 4:30 PM – 9:15 PM Entry Fee: Free

Info: A grand 16th-century wooden palace, once the seat of the Travancore Kingdom. Opening Hours: 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM (Closed on Mondays) Entry Fee: ₹25 (Indians), ₹300 (Foreigners)

Book Now
